🍝 Lasagna

A classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and melted cheese.

Description

Lasagna is warm, filling, and perfect for family dinners. Every layer brings together pasta, sauce, cheese, and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbly.
  5. Let cool before serving.
