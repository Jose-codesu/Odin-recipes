Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and tomato sauce.

This recipe is warm, filling, and perfect for a family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbly.
  5. Let it cool before serving.
