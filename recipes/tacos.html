🌮 Tacos

Delicious Mexican tacos filled with flavorful meat and fresh toppings.

Description

Tacos are simple, customizable, and packed with flavor. Perfect for quick meals and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add meat to the tortillas.
  4. Add toppings.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
