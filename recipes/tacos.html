Tacos

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made with tortillas filled with meat, vegetables, and toppings.

They are simple, delicious, and easy to customize.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add the meat to the tortillas.
  4. Add toppings.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
