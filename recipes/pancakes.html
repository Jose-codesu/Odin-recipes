Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast food made from a simple batter and cooked on a hot pan.

They are soft, fluffy, and usually served with syrup or fruit.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Stir until combined.
  4. Cook the batter on a hot pan.
  5. Flip and cook the other side.
