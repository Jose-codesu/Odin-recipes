🥞 Pancakes

Soft and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Description

Classic homemade pancakes with a soft texture and delicious flavor.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk and eggs.
  3. Stir until combined.
  4. Cook on a hot pan.
  5. Flip and serve.
